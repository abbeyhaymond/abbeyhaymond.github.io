The Patriots have been a powerhouse in the NFL for the past two decades. They have won six Super Bowl titles, and they looked like a team that was ready to claim another one during their 2017 season. Tom Brady had the most passing yards in a Super Bowl in 2018, so why was he not able to lead his team to a victory?

I started by looking into how the Patriots compared on offense to other Super Bowl losers from this century. I looked at all the teams’ yards from scrimmage and the number of plays to see how much each team was able to move the ball during their Super Bowl appearance.

ggplot() +

The chart shows that the 2017 Patriots are an outlier. Their offense moved the ball more than the average loser in a Super Bowl, and they had a lot of opportunities to do something with the ball, considering the number of plays they had possession. We can see that the Patriots moved the ball, but were they effective when doing so? How did they compare in other areas to the Eagles?

The table shows how the Patriots did in comparison to the Eagles on 3rd and 4th downs. Despite the Patriots not being as successful on 3rd and 4th downs, they still competed decently on offense. Maybe there was a bigger problem than Tom Brady and his offense. I looked at how their yards on defense in the Super Bowl compared to their entire season.

ggplot(nedefchart, aes(x=reorder(Week, Yards), weight=Yards, fill=Type)) +

So, here we go. The Patriots allowed more yards on defense than any other game that season. They allowed 164 rushing yards and 374 passing yards, struggling to stop the Eagles on the ground or through the air. If you look into it, you will find that the Patriot’s worst three games in terms of defensive yards allowed were all losses.

While the Patriots may have performed like a winning team on offense, the Eagles did a better job handling their defense. Ultimately, this was enough to keep them from another Super Bowl ring. The game was a matchup on offense, but the Patriots couldn’t keep the Eagle’s offense off the field when it needed to.
